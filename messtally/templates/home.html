About Us

Smart Mess is a web app to digitalize the Hostel Mess of colleges. It is a helping hand to students as well as the mess staff. Being a User Friendly platform it enables the staff members to easily keep a track of extra items consumed by the students and thereby create mess bill easily. The students are also notified about the items consumed by them by a message.


Features

  • Easy to Use

  • Removes Discrepancies in the Bill

  • Digitalises the mess
